About Me

Computer Science

  • Theoretical computer scientist.
    • Specializations in computability theory and programming language theory.
  • Programmer in over fifteen languages.
    • C, C++, C#, F#, Haskell, Node.js, JavaScript, Common Lisp, SmileBASIC, Visual Basic, ARM Assembly, CIL Assembly, x86 Assembly, Java, Python, and Scala.
  • Special skills in many popular paradigms of programming.
    • Familiar with functional programming, object-oriented programming, and parallel programming, among others.
  • Have written multiple programming languages.
    • An object-oriented language called N# which transpiles to SmileBASIC, a purely-function language called H# which runs on an interpreter, and even an extention of the pbrain programming language called Unlimibrain.
  • I also wrote a minimalistic clone of Minecraft for the Nintendo DSi.

Philosophy

  • One of those guys who believes Kant when it comes to metaphysics and epistemology.
    • Besides being familiar with transcendental idealism, also familiar with rationalism and empiricism.

  • Familiar with intelligence analysis.
